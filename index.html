Features

Easy access with QR code

Customers can easily view your menu using the QR code placed at the entrance or at the tables inside the restaurant. Links can also be provided to customers to view the menu.

Works on all devices

Presto Menu can be opened on any device. Everyone can open the menu directly on their own smartphone. You can view the menu instantly, you do not need to download or install an app.

Clean, safe and green

With the outbreak of Coronavirus, digital menus become a safer alternative to paper menus, since you avoid that multiple people touches the same thing during the meal. A digital menu removes paper from the restaurant workflow, saving the trees.

Online, Simple and always updated

Restaurants can create and update their digital menu directly, at any time, cutting the cost and offering a better menu, that is always updated and that can be improved over time. The menu is dynamic and can also include daily menus or new products, without an additional piece of paper. The menu is online and always accessible: it can be read from any location, at any time. You can also add a link to it from any website or app.

About

Restaurants and other eating establishments use Presto Digital Menu to create and publish beautiful menus online. Using Presto Menu you can easily create an online menu for your restaurant and allow your customers to view your digital menu directly. Through a QR code or a simple link that you share with your customers, they can view the menu on their smartphone, tablet or computer, whether they are in your restaurant or at home. Presto Menu aims to replace all paper menus with a better menu, which is online, accessible, green, clean, easy to update, and conveys more information and photos.
